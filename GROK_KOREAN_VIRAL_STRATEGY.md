## PART 1: Korean Market "Herd Mentality" (우르르 현상)

### The Phenomenon
In Korea, when something becomes trendy:
- Everyone rushes to try it (우르르 몰림)
- Social proof is extremely powerful ("다들 쓰니까 나도")
- FOMO drives adoption ("너 아직도 이거 안 써?")
- Spread is faster than Western markets (dense population + active SNS)

### Examples
| Product | What Happened |
|---------|---------------|
| 포켓몬빵 (Pokemon Bread) | Sold out nationwide, people hunting for it |
| 탕후루 (Tanghulu) | Suddenly everywhere, massive trend |
| 당근마켓 (Karrot) | Word of mouth → #1 secondhand app |
| 토스 (Toss) | "Easy money transfer" → Everyone uses it |

### The Pattern
```
Phase 1: Nobody knows (0 → 100 users)     [HARD - requires spark]
Phase 2: Small buzz (100 → 1,000)          [Needs social proof]
Phase 3: "Everyone's using it" (1K → 100K) [EASY - self-sustaining]
Phase 4: Mainstream (100K+)                [Automatic]
```

**The bottleneck is Phase 1 → Phase 2.** Once you cross into "buzz" territory, Korean market dynamics take over.
